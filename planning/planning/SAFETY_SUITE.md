# UpNext: Safety Suite Specifications

## 1. Verified Professional Status
* **Identity Encryption:** Entertainer legal IDs are encrypted and used only for verification; stage names are the only public identifiers.
* **Club Verification:** GPS-based check-ins ensure the entertainer is actually on-site before their status shows as "Active" to patrons.

## 2. The "Safe-Check" Timer
* **Active Monitoring:** When an entertainer starts a set, a customizable timer begins.
* **One-Tap Check-In:** At the end of a set or at set intervals, the entertainer taps a notification to confirm they are safe.
* **Escalation Protocol:** If a check-in is missed, the app triggers a silent alert to designated "Trusted Contacts" or on-site club management.

## 3. Discrepancy Reporting
* **Patron Flagging:** A private system for entertainers to flag problematic behavior from specific accounts, creating a shared "Safety Rating" visible only to other entertainers.
* **Secure Exit:** A "Request Escort" feature that sends a discreet signal to club security when an entertainer is ready to leave for their vehicle.

## 4. Privacy Shield
* **Blur Location:** Patrons can see which *club* an entertainer is at, but never their precise GPS coordinates within or outside the venue.
