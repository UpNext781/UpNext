# UpNext User Flows

## 1. The Entertainer Flow (The "Check-In")
* **Step 1: Authenticate.** Secure login via phone/email.
* **Step 2: Club Selection.** The entertainer selects their current location (GPS-verified).
* **Step 3: Set Status.** They toggle "Active" or "On Stage."
* **Step 4: Safety Check.** A "Safety Timer" begins. If not checked within a set window, a trusted contact is notified.

## 2. The Patron Flow (The "What's On")
* **Step 1: Discovery.** Open the app to see a map or list of nearby partner clubs.
* **Step 2: Lineup View.** Click a club to see who is "UpNext" and who is currently performing.
* **Step 3: Follow/Notify.** The patron "Stars" an entertainer to get a notification when they are 15 minutes from hitting the stage.
* **Step 4: Visit.** Integration with rideshare (Uber/Lyft) to get to the club directly.
